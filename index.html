Pain Points

The Struggle of Every Candidate & BLO

Managing electoral data in India shouldn't be a battle against paperwork. Legacy systems are holding back the future of governance.

"Every election I receive hundreds of PDF files from ECI. The data is there but I cannot do anything with it — I can't search, I can't sort, I can't filter. It's just images on paper." — Ramesh Kumar, Block Level Officer (BLO), Rampur UP
The BLO's Daily Struggle

Ramesh is a Block Level Officer responsible for managing voter data across his constituency in Uttar Pradesh. Every election cycle, ECI sends him 400+ PDF files — scanned images of voter lists in Hindi.

He needs to help candidates identify voters by age group, house, and category. But the data is completely rigid — stuck in image PDFs that no computer can read. Meanwhile candidates pay agents ₹4,00,000 just to digitize data that the government already gave for free.
